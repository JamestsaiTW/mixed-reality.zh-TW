<span data-ttu-id="cf11b-111">與全像攝影耳機中轉譯的內容相比，以沉浸式耳機轉譯的內容會以視覺化方式呈現。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cf11b-111">Content rendered in immersive headsets will appear visually different when compared to content rendered in holographic headsets.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="cf11b-112">雖然沉浸式耳機通常會以2D 螢幕上的預期來轉譯內容，但 HoloLens 這類的全像攝影耳機會使用色彩順序，請參閱以 RGB 顯示呈現全息影像。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cf11b-112">While immersive headsets generally render content much as you would expect on a 2D screen, holographic headsets like HoloLens use color-sequential, see-through RGB displays to renders holograms.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="cf11b-113">總是花時間在全像耳機中測試您的全像攝影體驗。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cf11b-113">Always take time to test your holographic experiences in a holographic headset.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="cf11b-114">內容的外觀（即使是專為全像攝影裝置所建立），在次要監視器、快照和 spectator view 中的顯示方式會有所不同。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cf11b-114">The appearance of content, even if it is built specifically for holographic devices, will differ as seen on secondary monitors, snapshots, and in spectator view.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="cf11b-115">別忘了使用裝置的經驗、測試全息影像的光線，以及從所有邊（以及從上方和下方）觀察內容的呈現方式。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cf11b-115">Remember to walk around experiences with a device, testing the lighting of holograms and observing from all sides (as well as from above and below) how your content is rendered.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="cf11b-116">請務必在裝置上的亮度設定範圍進行測試，因為不太可能所有的使用者都會共用假設的預設值，以及一組不同的光源條件。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cf11b-116">Be sure to test at a range of brightness settings on the device, as it is unlikely all users will share an assumed default, as well as a diverse set of lighting conditions.</span></span>

## <a name="fundamentals-of-rendering-on-holographic-devices"></a><span data-ttu-id="cf11b-117">在全像攝影裝置上呈現的基本概念</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cf11b-117">Fundamentals of rendering on holographic devices</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="cf11b-118">全像攝影**裝置具有 [加法] 顯示器**–從真實世界將光線新增到光線來建立全像投影，白色會顯示為明亮，而黑色則會透明。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cf11b-118">**Holographic devices have additive displays** – Holograms are created by adding light to the light from the real world – white will appear brightly, while black will appear transparent.</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="cf11b-119">**色彩影響會因使用者的環境而異**–使用者的房間內有許多不同的光源狀況。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cf11b-119">**Colors impact varies with the user’s environment** – There are many diverse lighting conditions in a user’s room.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="cf11b-120">建立具有適當對比層級的內容，以利清楚地協助。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cf11b-120">Create content with appropriate levels of contrast to help with clarity.</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="cf11b-121">**避免動態光源**–全像全像攝影的全像攝影，最有效率。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cf11b-121">**Avoid dynamic lighting** – Holograms that are uniformly lit in holographic experiences are the most efficient.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="cf11b-122">使用 advanced、dynamic 光源可能會超過行動裝置的功能。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cf11b-122">Using advanced, dynamic lighting will likely exceed the capabilities of mobile devices.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="cf11b-123">需要動態光源時，建議使用[混合現實工具組標準著色器](https://github.com/microsoft/MixedRealityToolkit-Unity/blob/mrtk_release/Documentation/README_MRTKStandardShader.md)。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cf11b-123">When dynamic lighting is required, it is recommended to use the [Mixed Reality Toolkit Standard shader](https://github.com/microsoft/MixedRealityToolkit-Unity/blob/mrtk_release/Documentation/README_MRTKStandardShader.md).</span></span> 

## <a name="designing-with-color"></a><span data-ttu-id="cf11b-124">使用色彩進行設計</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cf11b-124">Designing with color</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="cf11b-125">由於加總顯示的本質，某些色彩在全像攝影顯示器上可能會有所不同。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cf11b-125">Due to the nature of additive displays, certain colors can appear different on holographic displays.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="cf11b-126">有些色彩會在光源環境中彈出，有些則會顯示為較不影響力。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cf11b-126">Some colors will pop in lighting environments while others will appear as less impactful.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="cf11b-127">酷炫色彩通常會 recede 到背景，而暖色彩會跳至前景。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cf11b-127">Cool colors tend to recede into the background while warm colors jump to the foreground.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="cf11b-128">當您探索體驗的色彩時，請考慮下列因素：</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cf11b-128">Consider these factors as you explore color in your experiences:</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="cf11b-129">**色域**-HoloLens 從「寬範圍」的色彩中獲益，概念上類似于 Adobe RGB。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cf11b-129">**Gamut** - HoloLens benefits from a "wide gamut" of color, conceptually similar to Adobe RGB.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="cf11b-130">因此，某些色彩可能會在裝置中呈現不同的品質和標記法。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cf11b-130">As a result, some colors can exhibit different qualities and representation in the device.</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="cf11b-131">**Gamma** -呈現影像的亮度和對比會因沉浸式和全像攝影裝置而異。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cf11b-131">**Gamma** - The brightness and contrast of the rendered image will vary between immersive and holographic devices.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="cf11b-132">這些裝置的差異通常會顯示色彩和陰影的深色區域、更多或較不明亮。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cf11b-132">These device differences often appear to make dark areas of color and shadows, more or less bright.</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="cf11b-133">**色彩分隔**-也稱為 "color 並劃分" 或 "color fringing"，當使用者以眼睛追蹤物件時，移動全息影像（包括游標）最常發生的色彩區分。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cf11b-133">**Color separation** - Also called "color breakup" or "color fringing", color separation most commonly occurs with moving holograms (including cursor) when a user tracks objects with their eyes.</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="cf11b-134">**色彩一致性**-通常會將全像投影轉譯成明亮，使其維持色彩一致性（不論背景為何）。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cf11b-134">**Color uniformity** - Typically holograms are rendered brightly enough so that they maintain color uniformity, regardless of the background.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="cf11b-135">大型區域可能會變得 blotchy。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cf11b-135">Large areas may become blotchy.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="cf11b-136">避免出現明亮、純色的大型區域。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cf11b-136">Avoid large regions of bright, solid color.</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="cf11b-137">**呈現淺色**-白色外觀非常明亮，應謹慎使用。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cf11b-137">**Rendering light colors** - White appears very bright and should be used sparingly.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="cf11b-138">在大部分情況下，請考慮使用與 R 235 G 235 B 235 有關的白色值。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cf11b-138">For most cases, consider a white value around R 235 G 235 B 235.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="cf11b-139">較大的區域可能會造成使用者 discomfort。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cf11b-139">Large bright areas may cause user discomfort.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="cf11b-140">**呈現深色色彩**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cf11b-140">**Rendering dark colors**</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="cf11b-141">由於加總顯示的本質，暗色調會顯示為透明。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cf11b-141">Due to the nature of additive displays, dark colors appear transparent.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="cf11b-142">實心的黑色物件與真實世界的外觀並無不同。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cf11b-142">A solid black object will appear no different from the real world.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="cf11b-143">請參閱下方的 Alpha 色板。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cf11b-143">See Alpha channel below.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="cf11b-144">若要讓「黑色」的外觀嘗試使用非常深的灰階 RGB 值，例如16、16、16。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cf11b-144">To give the appearance of “black” try a very dark grey RGB value such as 16,16,16.</span></span>

## <a name="technical-considerations"></a><span data-ttu-id="cf11b-147">技術考慮</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cf11b-147">Technical considerations</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="cf11b-148">**別名**-明智失真、不規則或「樓梯步驟」，其中全息圖形幾何的邊緣會符合真實世界。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cf11b-148">**Aliasing** - Be considerate of aliasing, jagged or “stair steps” where the edge of a hologram’s geometry meets the real world.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="cf11b-149">使用具有高細節的材質可以 aggravate 此效果。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cf11b-149">Using textures with high detail can aggravate this effect.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="cf11b-150">材質應該對應並啟用篩選。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cf11b-150">Textures should be mapped and filtering enabled.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="cf11b-151">請考慮將全息影像的邊緣漸淡，或加入在物件周圍建立黑色邊緣框線的材質。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cf11b-151">Consider fading the edges of holograms or adding a texture that creates a black edge border around objects.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="cf11b-152">盡可能避免精簡型幾何。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cf11b-152">Avoid thin geometry where possible.</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="cf11b-153">**Alpha**色板-您必須針對未轉譯全息影像的任何零件，清除您的 Alpha 色板以完全透明。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cf11b-153">**Alpha channel** - You must clear your alpha channel to fully transparent for any parts where you are not rendering a hologram.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="cf11b-154">當您從裝置或使用 Spectator View 取得影像/影片時，將 Alpha 未定義的會導致視覺效果構件。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cf11b-154">Leaving the alpha undefined leads to visual artifacts when taking images/videos from the device or with Spectator View.</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="cf11b-155">**材質軟化**-由於光線是全像投影顯示器的加法，因此最好避免較大的明亮色彩，因為它們通常不會產生想要的視覺效果。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cf11b-155">**Texture softening** - Since light is additive in holographic displays, it is best to avoid large regions of bright, solid color as they often do not produce the intended visual effect.</span></span>
