<span data-ttu-id="01af9-175">這會稍微複雜一點。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01af9-175">This is a little more tricky.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="01af9-176">讓全像投影的「旋轉」按鈕變得更豐富的體驗。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01af9-176">Having holographic “rotate” buttons to tap makes for a terrible experience.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="01af9-177">沒有可運用的實體控制項, 因此行為必須來自于代表 lander 的物件或 lander 本身的操作。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01af9-177">There isn’t a physical control to leverage, so the behavior must come from manipulation of an object representing the lander, or with the lander itself.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="01af9-178">我們提出了使用點按和拖曳的方法, 可讓使用者以他們想要的方向有效地「推送並提取」。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01af9-178">We came up with a method using tap-and-drag which enables a user to effectively “push and pull” it in the direction they want it to face.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="01af9-179">每當使用者按下並按住時, 筆勢起始的空間就會變成旋轉的原點。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01af9-179">Any time a user taps and holds, the point in space where the gesture was initiated becomes the origin for rotation.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="01af9-180">從原點拖曳會轉換手轉譯的差異 (X、Y、Z), 並將其套用至 lander 旋轉值的差異。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01af9-180">Dragging from the origin converts the delta of the hand's translation (X,Y,Z) and applies it to the delta of the lander's rotation values.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="01af9-181">更簡單地說, 向*左拖曳 <-> right, 向上 <-> 向下、向前 <-在空格中向後 > 會相應旋轉出貨*。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01af9-181">Or more simply, *dragging left <-> right, up <-> down, forward <-> back in spaces rotates the ship accordingly*.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="01af9-182">因為 HoloLens 可以追蹤兩個手, 所以可以在天生由左側控制時, 將旋轉指派給右手邊。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01af9-182">Since the HoloLens can track two hands, rotation can be assigned to the right hand while thrust is controlled by the left.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="01af9-183">Finesse 是此遊戲中成功的驅動因素。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01af9-183">Finesse is the driving factor for success in this game.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="01af9-184">這些互動的*風格*是絕對最高的優先順序。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01af9-184">The *feel* of these interactions is the absolute highest priority.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="01af9-185">尤其是在觸覺深度的內容中。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01af9-185">Especially in context of tactile immersion.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="01af9-186">有太多反應的出貨會不必要地進行操作, 而速度太慢時, 使用者必須在出貨時「推送並提取」一段滑雪的時間。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01af9-186">A ship that reacts too quickly would be unnecessarily difficult to steer, while one too slow would require the user to “push and pull” on the ship for an awkwardly long amount of time.</span></span>

### <a name="adding-input-for-game-controllers"></a><span data-ttu-id="01af9-187">新增遊戲控制器的輸入</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01af9-187">Adding input for game controllers</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="01af9-188">雖然 HoloLens 上的手勢提供了微調控制項的 novel 方法, 但還是有一些從類比控制項取得的「true」觸覺意見反應。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01af9-188">While hand gestures on the HoloLens provide a novel method of fine-grain control, there is still a certain lack of 'true' tactile feedback that you get from analog controls.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="01af9-189">連接 Xbox 遊戲控制器可讓您回頭瞭解這種 physicality, 同時利用控制杆來保留精細的控制。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01af9-189">Connecting an Xbox game controller allows to bring back this sense of physicality while leveraging the control sticks to retain fine-grain control.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="01af9-190">有多種方式可將相對直接的控制項配置套用至 Xbox 控制器。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01af9-190">There are multiple ways to apply the relatively straight-forward control scheme to the Xbox controller.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="01af9-191">由於我們正嘗試盡可能接近原始 arcade 設定, 因此**天生**對應最適合 [觸發程式] 按鈕。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01af9-191">Since we're trying to stay as close to the original arcade set up as possible, **Thrust** maps best to the trigger button.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="01af9-192">這些按鈕是類比控制項, 這表示它們的 [*開啟] 和 [關閉*] 狀態都很簡單, 它們實際上會回應壓力的程度。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01af9-192">These buttons are analog controls, meaning they have more than simple *on and off* states, they actually respond to the degree of pressure put on them.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="01af9-193">這會提供類似的結構做為**天生杠杆**。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01af9-193">This gives us a similar construct as the **thrust lever**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="01af9-194">不同于原始遊戲和手勢, 此控制項會在使用者停止觸發程式的壓力時, 剪下出貨的天生。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01af9-194">Unlike the original game and the hand gesture, this control will cut the ship's thrust once a user stops putting pressure on the trigger.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="01af9-195">它仍然會為使用者提供與原始 arcade 遊戲相同程度的 finesse。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01af9-195">It still gives the user the same degree of finesse as the original arcade game did.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="01af9-196">![左側的操縱杆會對應至偏擺和變換, 右杆會對應至音調並變換](images/thumbsticksidebyside.gif)</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01af9-196">![Left thumbstick is mapped to Yaw and Roll, Right thumbstick is mapped to Pitch and Roll](images/thumbsticksidebyside.gif)</span></span><br>
<span data-ttu-id="01af9-197">*左側的操縱杆會對應到偏擺並變換;右側的操縱杆會對應至音調並變換*</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01af9-197">*Left thumbstick is mapped to yaw and roll; right thumbstick is mapped to pitch and roll*</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="01af9-198">雙重 thumbsticks 自然就能控制出貨的旋轉。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01af9-198">The dual thumbsticks naturally lend themselves to controlling ship rotation.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="01af9-199">可惜的是, 出貨可以旋轉3個軸, 兩個 thumbsticks 都支援兩個軸。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01af9-199">Unfortunately, there are 3 axes on which the ship can rotate and two thumbsticks which both support two axes.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="01af9-200">這種不相符的意思是一個操縱杆控制一個軸;或是 thumbsticks 的座標軸重迭。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01af9-200">This mismatch means either one thumbstick controls one axis; or there is overlap of axes for the thumbsticks.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="01af9-201">先前的解決方案最終感覺「中斷」, 因為 thumbsticks 原本就會混合其本機 X 和 Y 值。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01af9-201">The former solution ended up feeling "broken" since thumbsticks inherently blend their local X and Y values.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="01af9-202">後者的解決方案需要進行一些測試, 以找出哪些重複的軸覺得最自然。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01af9-202">The latter solution required some testing to find which redundant axes feel the most natural.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="01af9-203">最後一個範例會針對左側的操縱杆使用*偏擺*和變換 (Y 和 X 軸), 並針對右側的操縱杆使用*音調*和變換 (Z 和 X 軸)。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01af9-203">The final sample uses *yaw* and *roll* (Y and X axes) for the left thumbstick, and *pitch* and *roll* (Z and X axes) for the right thumbstick.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="01af9-204">這覺得最自然的是 , 隨著*偏擺*和*螺距*的變換也是獨立配對。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01af9-204">This felt the most natural as *roll* seems to independently pair well with *yaw* and *pitch*.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="01af9-205">就這一點而言, 同時使用 thumbsticks 進行變換也會發生兩倍的旋轉值;讓 lander do 迴圈變得非常有趣。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01af9-205">As a side note, using both thumbsticks for *roll* also happens to double the rotation value; it's pretty fun to have the lander do loops.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="01af9-206">這個範例應用程式會示範空間辨識和觸覺深度如何大幅改變體驗, 因為這是 Windows Mixed Reality 的可擴充輸入形式。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01af9-206">This sample app demonstrates how spatial recognition and tactile immersion can significantly change an experience thanks to Windows Mixed Reality's extensible input modalities.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="01af9-207">雖然農曆 Lander 的年齡可能接近40年, 但使用該小型的八邊形所公開的概念也會永久存留。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01af9-207">While Lunar Lander may be nearing 40 years in age, the concepts exposed with that little octagon-with-legs will live on forever.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="01af9-208">想像未來, 為什麼看不到過去？</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01af9-208">When imagining the future, why not look at the past?</span></span>
