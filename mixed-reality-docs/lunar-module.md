<span data-ttu-id="5d0bb-175">這是有點棘手。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5d0bb-175">This is a little more tricky.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="5d0bb-176">有全像攝影版"旋轉"的按鈕來點選讓可怕的體驗。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5d0bb-176">Having holographic “rotate” buttons to tap makes for a terrible experience.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="5d0bb-177">沒有實體控制項來運用，因此操作或處理 lander 本身的物件，代表 lander，都必須來自行為。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5d0bb-177">There isn’t a physical control to leverage, so the behavior must come from manipulation of an object representing the lander, or with the lander itself.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="5d0bb-178">我們想出了使用點選並拖曳這讓使用者能夠有效地 「 推送和提取 」 方法方向在他們想要面對。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5d0bb-178">We came up with a method using tap-and-drag which enables a user to effectively “push and pull” it in the direction they want it to face.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="5d0bb-179">每當使用者點選，而且會保存，起始筆勢所在的空間中的點會變成旋轉的原點。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5d0bb-179">Any time a user taps and holds, the point in space where the gesture was initiated becomes the origin for rotation.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="5d0bb-180">從來源將手的翻譯 （X，Y，Z） 的差異，並套用至 lander 旋轉值的差異。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5d0bb-180">Dragging from the origin converts the delta of the hand's translation (X,Y,Z) and applies it to the delta of the lander's rotation values.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="5d0bb-181">或者形式更簡單*空間中的上一步拖曳左側 <>-權限，註冊 <>-向下]、 [下一頁 <>-據以旋轉太空船*。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5d0bb-181">Or more simply, *dragging left <-> right, up <-> down, forward <-> back in spaces rotates the ship accordingly*.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="5d0bb-182">因為 HoloLens 可以追蹤兩個實際操作，旋轉可以指派給右手邊時主要目的是受到左邊。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5d0bb-182">Since the HoloLens can track two hands, rotation can be assigned to the right hand while thrust is controlled by the left.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="5d0bb-183">採取一些手段是驅動因素在這個遊戲中的成功。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5d0bb-183">Finesse is the driving factor for success in this game.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="5d0bb-184">*覺得*這些互動的是絕對的最高優先順序。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5d0bb-184">The *feel* of these interactions is the absolute highest priority.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="5d0bb-185">尤其是在 tactile 訓練活動的內容。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5d0bb-185">Especially in context of tactile immersion.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="5d0bb-186">回應速度太快出貨不必要地難以可以操縱，雖然速度太慢的其中一個會要求使用者要 「 推送和提取 」 在太空船姿長的時間量。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5d0bb-186">A ship that reacts too quickly would be unnecessarily difficult to steer, while one too slow would require the user to “push and pull” on the ship for an awkwardly long amount of time.</span></span>

### <a name="adding-input-for-game-controllers"></a><span data-ttu-id="5d0bb-187">正在加入輸入遊戲控制器</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5d0bb-187">Adding input for game controllers</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="5d0bb-188">HoloLens 上的手勢提供嶄新的細微的控制方法，但仍 'true' tactile 的意見反應，您從類比控制項取得缺乏。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5d0bb-188">While hand gestures on the HoloLens provide a novel method of fine-grain control, there is still a certain lack of 'true' tactile feedback that you get from analog controls.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="5d0bb-189">連接 Xbox 遊戲控制器可讓這種情況下的 physicality 找回同時還能利用控制項隨身碟，保留細微的控制。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5d0bb-189">Connecting an Xbox game controller allows to bring back this sense of physicality while leveraging the control sticks to retain fine-grain control.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="5d0bb-190">有多種方式來套用相對較簡單的控制項配置至 Xbox 控制器。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5d0bb-190">There are multiple ways to apply the relatively straight-forward control scheme to the Xbox controller.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="5d0bb-191">因為我們想要保持盡可能的情況下，設定原始 arcade 靠近**推力**最佳對應至觸發程序 按鈕。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5d0bb-191">Since we're trying to stay as close to the original arcade set up as possible, **Thrust** maps best to the trigger button.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="5d0bb-192">這些按鈕是類比的控制項，這表示它們有多個簡單*開啟和關閉*所述，它們實際回應放在其上的壓力程度。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5d0bb-192">These buttons are analog controls, meaning they have more than simple *on and off* states, they actually respond to the degree of pressure put on them.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="5d0bb-193">這會提供類似的建構，當做**推力桿**。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5d0bb-193">This gives us a similar construct as the **thrust lever**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="5d0bb-194">不同於原始遊戲和手動動作，此控制項將剪下 ship 的主要目的是之後使用者就會停止將壓力放在觸發程序。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5d0bb-194">Unlike the original game and the hand gesture, this control will cut the ship's thrust once a user stops putting pressure on the trigger.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="5d0bb-195">它仍可讓使用者相同程度的採取一些手段原始 arcade 遊戲一樣。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5d0bb-195">It still gives the user the same degree of finesse as the original arcade game did.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="5d0bb-196">![左搖桿會對應至偏航和回復，右搖桿會對應到講述並回復](images/thumbsticksidebyside.gif)</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5d0bb-196">![Left thumbstick is mapped to Yaw and Roll, Right thumbstick is mapped to Pitch and Roll](images/thumbsticksidebyside.gif)</span></span><br>
<span data-ttu-id="5d0bb-197">*左搖桿會對應至偏航和回復;右搖桿會對應至推銷和復原*</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5d0bb-197">*Left thumbstick is mapped to yaw and roll; right thumbstick is mapped to pitch and roll*</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="5d0bb-198">雙重搖桿本身來控制的出貨旋轉。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5d0bb-198">The dual thumbsticks naturally lend themselves to controlling ship rotation.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="5d0bb-199">不幸的是，有 3 個座標軸上的出貨可以旋轉和這兩個支援的兩個軸的兩個搖桿。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5d0bb-199">Unfortunately, there are 3 axes on which the ship can rotate and two thumbsticks which both support two axes.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="5d0bb-200">這種不符合表示任一一搖桿控制項一個軸;或搖桿座標軸的重疊。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5d0bb-200">This mismatch means either one thumbstick controls one axis; or there is overlap of axes for the thumbsticks.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="5d0bb-201">第一種解決方案最終會覺得 「 已中斷 」，因為搖桿原本就是 blend 當地 X 和 Y 值。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5d0bb-201">The former solution ended up feeling "broken" since thumbsticks inherently blend their local X and Y values.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="5d0bb-202">第二種解決方案需要某些測試以查明哪些多餘的軸覺得最自然。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5d0bb-202">The latter solution required some testing to find which redundant axes feel the most natural.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="5d0bb-203">最後一個範例會使用*偏航*並*向前復原*（Y 軸和 X 軸） 的左搖桿，和*音調*和*彙*（Z 軸和 X 軸） 提供的權限搖桿。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5d0bb-203">The final sample uses *yaw* and *roll* (Y and X axes) for the left thumbstick, and *pitch* and *roll* (Z and X axes) for the right thumbstick.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="5d0bb-204">這認為一樣最自然*回復*似乎也與獨立配對*偏航*和*字幅*。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5d0bb-204">This felt the most natural as *roll* seems to independently pair well with *yaw* and *pitch*.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="5d0bb-205">附帶一提，使用的兩個搖桿*向前復原*也會發生循環值中; 的兩倍就有 do 迴圈 lander 很有趣。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5d0bb-205">As a side note, using both thumbsticks for *roll* also happens to double the rotation value; it's pretty fun to have the lander do loops.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="5d0bb-206">此範例應用程式示範如何空間辨識和 tactile 訓練活動可能會大幅改變感謝 Windows Mixed Reality 的可延伸的輸入形式的體驗。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5d0bb-206">This sample app demonstrates how spatial recognition and tactile immersion can significantly change an experience thanks to Windows Mixed Reality's extensible input modalities.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="5d0bb-207">雖然陰曆 Lander 可能即將在時代的 40 年，概念以公開與 legs 小八邊形-將 live 下去。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5d0bb-207">While Lunar Lander may be nearing 40 years in age, the concepts exposed with that little octagon-with-legs will live on forever.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="5d0bb-208">當想像在未來，為何不會看到過去？</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5d0bb-208">When imagining the future, why not look at the past?</span></span>
