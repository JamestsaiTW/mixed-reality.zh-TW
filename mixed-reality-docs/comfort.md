## <a name="vergence-accommodation-conflict"></a><span data-ttu-id="ae92a-111">Vergence-住宿衝突</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ae92a-111">Vergence-accommodation conflict</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="ae92a-112">若要清楚地觀賞物件, 人類必須[配合](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Accommodation_%28eye%29)物件的距離, 或將其眼睛的焦點調整至物件。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ae92a-112">To view objects clearly, humans must [accommodate](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Accommodation_%28eye%29), or adjust their eyes’ focus, to the distance of the object.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ae92a-113">同時, 這兩個眼睛的旋轉必須[收斂](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Convergence_(eye))于物件的距離, 以避免看到雙重影像。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ae92a-113">At the same time, the rotation of both eyes must [converge](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Convergence_(eye)) to the object’s distance to avoid seeing double images.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ae92a-114">在自然觀賞中, 會連結 vergence 和住宿。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ae92a-114">In natural viewing, vergence and accommodation are linked.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ae92a-115">當您觀賞附近的東西 (例如靠近鼻子的 housefly) 時, 您的眼睛會交叉並配合接近點。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ae92a-115">When you view something near (e.g. a housefly close to your nose) your eyes cross and accommodate to a near point.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ae92a-116">相反地, 如果您以光學無限大 (大約是從6m 開始, 或針對一般視覺) 來查看, 您的眼睛就會變得平行, 而您的眼睛鏡頭會被無限大。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ae92a-116">Conversely, if you view something at optical infinity (roughly starting at 6m or farther for normal vision), your eyes’ lines of sight become parallel and your eyes’ lenses accommodate to infinity.</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="ae92a-117">在大部分的前端掛接顯示器中, 使用者一律會配合顯示的邊緣距離 (以取得清晰的影像), 但會收斂到感興趣物件的距離 (以取得單一影像)。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ae92a-117">In most head-mounted displays users will always accommodate to the focal distance of the display (to get a sharp image), but converge to the distance of the object of interest (to get a single image).</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ae92a-118">當使用者配合並融合到不同的距離時, 兩個提示之間的自然連結必須中斷, 而這可能會導致視覺 discomfort 或疲勞。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ae92a-118">When users accommodate and converge to different distances, the natural link between the two cues must be broken and this can lead to visual discomfort or fatigue.</span></span>
