# <a name="what-is-mixed-reality"></a><span data-ttu-id="c65b4-104">什麼是混合現實？</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c65b4-104">What is mixed reality?</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="c65b4-105">混合現實是將實體世界與數位世界相結合的結果。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c65b4-105">Mixed reality is the result of blending the physical world with the digital world.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c65b4-106">混合的現實是人們、電腦和環境互動的下一次演進, 而且現在僅限於我們的 imaginations。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c65b4-106">Mixed reality is the next evolution in human, computer, and environment interaction and unlocks possibilities that before now were restricted to our imaginations.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c65b4-107">這是藉由電腦視覺、圖形處理能力、顯示器技術和輸入系統的進展而達成的。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c65b4-107">It is made possible by advancements in computer vision, graphical processing power, display technology, and input systems.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c65b4-108">「*混合現實*」一詞原本是由 Paul Milgram 和 Fumio Kishino 「[混合現實視覺效果的分類法](http://etclab.mie.utoronto.ca/people/paul_dir/IEICE94/ieice.html)」所引進的1994論文。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c65b4-108">The term *mixed reality* was originally introduced in a 1994 paper by Paul Milgram and Fumio Kishino, "[A Taxonomy of Mixed Reality Visual Displays](http://etclab.mie.utoronto.ca/people/paul_dir/IEICE94/ieice.html)."</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c65b4-109">其論文引進了*virtuality continuum*的概念, 並著重于如何套用分類法的分類。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c65b4-109">Their paper introduced the concept of the *virtuality continuum*, and focused on how the categorization of taxonomy applied to displays.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c65b4-110">從那時起, 混合現實的應用程式會超出顯示範圍。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c65b4-110">Since then, the application of mixed reality goes beyond displays.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c65b4-111">它也包含環境輸入、空間音效和位置。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c65b4-111">It also includes environmental input, spatial sound, and location.</span></span>

## <a name="environmental-input-and-perception"></a><span data-ttu-id="c65b4-112">環境輸入和認知</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c65b4-112">Environmental input and perception</span></span>

![顯示電腦、人類和環境之間互動的卞氏圖表](images/mixed-reality-venn-diagram-300px.png)<br> 

<span data-ttu-id="c65b4-114">過去幾年來, 人類與電腦輸入之間的關聯性已經過充分的探索。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c65b4-114">Over the past several decades, the relationship between human and computer input has been well explored.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c65b4-115">它甚至已經過廣泛研究的專業領域, 稱為*人類電腦互動*或 HCI。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c65b4-115">It even has a widely studied discipline known as *human computer interaction* or HCI.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c65b4-116">人類輸入會透過各種不同的方式進行, 包括鍵盤、滑鼠、觸控、筆墨、語音, 甚至是 Kinect 的框架追蹤。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c65b4-116">Human input happens through a variety of means, including keyboards, mice, touch, ink, voice, and even Kinect skeletal tracking.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="c65b4-117">在感應器和處理方面的改進, 是從環境逐漸增加到電腦輸入的新區域。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c65b4-117">Advancements in sensors and processing are giving rise to a new area of computer input from environments.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c65b4-118">電腦與環境之間的互動是有效的環境理解或*認知*。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c65b4-118">The interaction between computers and environments is effectively environmental understanding or *perception*.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c65b4-119">因此, Windows 中顯示環境資訊的 API 名稱稱為「[認知 api](https://docs.microsoft.com/uwp/api/Windows.Perception)」。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c65b4-119">Hence the API names in Windows that reveal environmental information are called the [perception APIs](https://docs.microsoft.com/uwp/api/Windows.Perception).</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c65b4-120">環境輸入會在世界中 (例如, [head 追蹤](coordinate-systems.md))、表面和邊界 (例如[空間對應](spatial-mapping.md)和[空間理解](case-study-expanding-the-spatial-mapping-capabilities-of-hololens.md))、環境光源、環境音效、物件等專案中, 捕捉到人員的職位辨識和位置。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c65b4-120">Environmental input captures things like a person's position in the world (e.g. [head tracking](coordinate-systems.md)), surfaces and boundaries (e.g. [spatial mapping](spatial-mapping.md) and [spatial understanding](case-study-expanding-the-spatial-mapping-capabilities-of-hololens.md)), ambient lighting, environmental sound, object recognition, and location.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="c65b4-121">現在, 所有三種電腦處理、人類輸入和環境輸入的組合, 都能為您創造出真正的混合現實體驗。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c65b4-121">Now, the combination of all three--computer processing, human input, and environmental input--sets the opportunity to create true mixed reality experiences.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c65b4-122">透過實體世界的移動可以轉譯成數位世界中的移動。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c65b4-122">Movement through the physical world can translate to movement in the digital world.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c65b4-123">實體世界的界限可能會影響數位世界中的應用程式體驗, 例如遊戲播放。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c65b4-123">Boundaries in the physical world can influence application experiences, such as game play, in the digital world.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c65b4-124">如果沒有環境輸入, 體驗就無法在實體和數位現實之間混合。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c65b4-124">Without environmental input, experiences cannot blend between physical and digital realities.</span></span>

## <a name="the-mixed-reality-spectrum"></a><span data-ttu-id="c65b4-125">混合現實頻譜</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c65b4-125">The mixed reality spectrum</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="c65b4-126">因為混合現實會混合實體和數位世界, 所以這兩個現實會定義一系列稱為 virtuality continuum 的極座標。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c65b4-126">Since mixed reality blends both physical and digital worlds, these two realities define the polar ends of a spectrum known as the virtuality continuum.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c65b4-127">為了簡單起見, 我們將此稱為「*混合現實」頻譜*。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c65b4-127">For simplicity, we refer to this as the *mixed reality spectrum*.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c65b4-128">在左側, 我們有實際的現實, 也就是我們的存在;在右手邊, 我們有對應的數位現實。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c65b4-128">On the left-hand side we have physical reality in which we, humans, exist; on the right-hand side we have the corresponding digital reality.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="c65b4-129">現今市場上大部分的行動電話幾乎都沒有環境理解功能。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c65b4-129">Most mobile phones on the market today have little to no environmental understanding capabilities.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c65b4-130">因此, 他們所提供的體驗無法在實體和數位現實之間混合。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c65b4-130">Thus the experiences they offer cannot mix between physical and digital realities.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c65b4-131">在實體世界的影片串流上重迭圖形的體驗, 會有更豐富的*現實*。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c65b4-131">The experiences that overlay graphics on video streams of the physical world are *augmented reality*.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c65b4-132">遮蔽您的觀點來呈現數位體驗的經驗, 就是*虛擬實境*。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c65b4-132">The experiences that occlude your view to present a digital experience are *virtual reality*.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c65b4-133">如您所見, 在這兩個極端之間啟用的經驗, 是*混合現實*的:</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c65b4-133">As you can see, the experiences enabled between these two extremes is *mixed reality*:</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="c65b4-134">從實體世界開始, 將數位物件 (例如全息的影像) 放在真正的處。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c65b4-134">Starting with the physical world, placing a digital object, such as a hologram, as if it was really there.</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="c65b4-135">從實體世界開始, 另一個人的數位標記法--[頭像]--顯示離開便箋時的位置。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c65b4-135">Starting with the physical world, a digital representation of another person--an avatar--shows the location where they were standing when leaving notes.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c65b4-136">換句話說, 在不同時間點表示非同步共同作業的經驗。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c65b4-136">In other words, experiences that represent asynchronous collaboration at different points in time.</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="c65b4-137">從數位世界開始, 實體世界 (例如牆和傢俱) 的實體界限會以數位方式出現在體驗中, 以協助使用者避免實體物件。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c65b4-137">Starting with a digital world, physical boundaries from the physical world, such as walls and furniture, appear digitally within the experience to help users avoid physical objects.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="c65b4-139">現今提供的大部分增強的現實和虛擬實境產品, 都代表此頻譜的一小部分。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c65b4-139">Most augmented reality and virtual reality offerings available today represent a very small part of this spectrum.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c65b4-140">不過, 它們也是較大混合現實頻譜的子集。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c65b4-140">They are, however, subsets of the larger mixed reality spectrum.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c65b4-141">Windows 10 以整個頻譜為基礎, 並可讓人、地點和事物的數位代表與真實世界混合。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c65b4-141">Windows 10 is built with the entire spectrum in mind, and allows blending digital representations of people, places, and things with the real world.</span></span>

![混合現實頻譜中的裝置類型](images/mixed-reality-spectrum-device-types-550px.png)

<span data-ttu-id="c65b4-143">有兩種主要的裝置類型可提供 Windows Mixed Reality 體驗:</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c65b4-143">There are two main types of devices that deliver Windows Mixed Reality experiences:</span></span>

## <a name="devices-and-experiences"></a><span data-ttu-id="c65b4-164">裝置和體驗</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c65b4-164">Devices and experiences</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="c65b4-165">技術進步是已啟用混合現實體驗的功能。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c65b4-165">Technological advancement is what has enabled mixed reality experiences.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c65b4-166">目前沒有任何可在整個頻譜中執行體驗的裝置。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c65b4-166">There are no devices today that can run experiences across the entire spectrum.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c65b4-167">不過, Windows 10 為裝置製造商和開發人員提供通用的混合現實平臺。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c65b4-167">However, Windows 10 provides a common mixed reality platform for both device manufacturers and developers.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c65b4-168">現今的裝置可以支援混合現實頻譜內的特定範圍。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c65b4-168">Devices today can support a specific range within the mixed reality spectrum.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c65b4-169">經過一段時間後, 新的裝置將會擴充該範圍。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c65b4-169">Over time, new devices will expand that range.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c65b4-170">未來, 全像攝影的裝置會變得更沉浸, 而沉浸式裝置會變得更好。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c65b4-170">In the future, holographic devices will become more immersive, and immersive devices will become more holographic.</span></span>
