<span data-ttu-id="d2781-111">任何設計程式或設計架構都是用來反復執行思考:若要廣泛地解決問題, 請與他人分享想法, 以評估這些想法並觸及解決方案。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d2781-111">Any design process or design framework is meant to iterate thinking: To approach a problem broadly, to share ideas with others effectively, to evaluate those ideas and reach a solution.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d2781-112">現在, 我們已建立良好的設計和開發方法, 可在桌上型電腦、手機和平板電腦上建立體驗。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d2781-112">Today, we have well-established design and development methods for building experiences on desktops, phones, and tablets.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d2781-113">小組對於反覆運算想法並將其轉換成使用者的產品, 有清楚的期望。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d2781-113">Teams have clear expectations of what is necessary to iterate an idea and turn it into a product for users.</span></span>
