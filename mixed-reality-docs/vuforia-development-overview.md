## <a name="extended-tracking-with-vuforia"></a><span data-ttu-id="7fcba-154">使用 Vuforia 擴充追蹤</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7fcba-154">Extended tracking with Vuforia</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="7fcba-155">[延伸追蹤](https://library.vuforia.com/articles/Training/Extended-Tracking)會建立環境的對應，以維持追蹤，即使目標已不在查看中也一樣。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7fcba-155">[Extended tracking](https://library.vuforia.com/articles/Training/Extended-Tracking) creates a map of the environment to maintain tracking even when a target is no longer in view.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7fcba-156">它是 HoloLens 所執行之空間對應的 Vuforia 引擎。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7fcba-156">It is Vuforia Engines’ counterpart to the spatial mapping performed by HoloLens.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7fcba-157">當您在目標上啟用延伸追蹤時，您可以讓該目標的姿勢傳遞至空間對應系統。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7fcba-157">When you enable extended tracking on a target, you enable the pose of that target to be passed to the spatial mapping system.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7fcba-158">如此一來，目標就可以同時存在於 Vuforia 引擎和 HoloLens 空間座標系統中，但不會同時存在。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7fcba-158">In this way, targets can exist in both the Vuforia Engine and HoloLens spatial coordinate systems, though not simultaneously.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="7fcba-166">Vuforia 的目標追蹤器可識別目標</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7fcba-166">Vuforia’s target Tracker recognizes the target</span></span>
