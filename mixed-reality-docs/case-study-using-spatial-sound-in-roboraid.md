<span data-ttu-id="141af-164">在具有完整範圍頻率的主控台遊戲上, 您有更多選項可以根據音效的重要性來細分頻率。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="141af-164">On a console game with full range of frequency you have more options to divide frequencies up depending on the importance of the sound.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="141af-165">不過, 在 RoboRaid 中, 我限制了我可以從音效彎曲的頻率範圍。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="141af-165">For RoboRaid, however, I was limited in the number of ranges of frequencies I could curve out from sounds.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="141af-166">比方說, 如果您使用低通濾波器, 並從範圍較高的曲線向外彎曲, 就不會有任何專案留在音效中, 因為沒有太多的低端。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="141af-166">For instance, if you use low-pass filter and curve out too much from the higher end of the spectrum, you won’t have anything left on the sound because there’s not much low-end.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="141af-167">為了讓 RoboRaid 聽起來像是裝置上的最大, 我們必須降低整個體驗的動態範圍, 並藉由為不同類型的音效建立清楚的重要性階層, 以充分運用回避。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="141af-167">In order to make RoboRaid sound as big as it can on the device, we had to lower the dynamic range of the whole experience and made extensive use of ducking by creating a clear hierarchy of importance for different types of sounds.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="141af-168">我根據重要性設定-2 到-6 資料庫的回避。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="141af-168">I set the ducking from -2 to -6 dB depending on the importance.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="141af-169">我個人不喜歡遊戲中的明顯回避, 因此我花了很多時間微調淡入/縮小時間和磁片區衰減量。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="141af-169">I personally don’t like obvious ducking in games, so I spent a lot of time tuning the fade in/out timing and the amount of volume attenuation.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="141af-170">我們為空間音效、非空間音效、VO 和試匯流排設定不同的匯流排, 而不會對音樂進行回音, 然後建立非常高的優先順序、關鍵和非重大匯流排。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="141af-170">We set up separate busses for spatial sound, non-spatial sound, VO, and dry bus without reverb for music, then created very high priority, critical, and non-critical busses.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="141af-171">然後, 我們會設定資產以移至適當的匯流排。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="141af-171">The assets were then set up to go to their appropriate busses.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="141af-172">我希望音訊專業人員能夠在自己的應用程式上運作, 因為我在 RoboRaid 上工作。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="141af-172">I hope audio professionals out there will have as much fun and excitement working on their own apps as I did working on RoboRaid.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="141af-173">我看不到 (聽過!)
